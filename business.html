Flow 1: User & AI Interaction

1. User Initiates Action

A business user connects their Web3 wallet and opens a micro-app on the platform.

(e.g., a sales manager creates a new client entry.)

2. Data & Metadata Capture

(A new "node" is created in the DAS.)

3. Micro-Transaction Payment

The action triggers a low-cost transaction on the SNET Blockchain, paying the developer.

‚Üí
4. AI-Powered Request

The user asks the AI to perform a complex task that requires context from multiple micro-apps.

(e.g., "Create a welcome email for this new client...")

‚Üí
5. Data-Driven AI Execution

The Hyperon AI accesses and synthesizes connected data from the SNET DAS.

6. AI Delivers Content/Action

The AI generates a personalized, context-aware email draft for the user.
